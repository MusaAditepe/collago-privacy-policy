Account Deletion Process

To delete your account in the Collago app, follow these steps:

Account Deletion Steps

1. In-App Settings: Open the Collago app and go to "Settings".
2. Account Management: Select "Account Management" or "Profile Settings".
3. Delete Account: Find and click "Delete Account" or "Close Account".
4. Confirmation: Confirm the account deletion in the popup window.
5. Email Verification: Click the verification link sent to your email address.

Data to be Deleted

Personal Information

  • Name, surname, username
  • Email address and phone number
  • Profile photo and biography
  • Age, gender and other profile information

App Content

  • All collages you created
  • Photos you shared
  • Comments and messages you wrote
  • Likes and interactions

Social Data

  • Friends list and connections
  • Group memberships
  • Users you follow
  • Social interaction history

Technical Data

  • App usage history
  • Device information
  • Location data (if any)
  • Log records

After Account Deletion

What Will Happen?

  • All your personal data will be deleted
  • Content you created will be removed
  • Friends list and connections will be deleted
  • Access to your app account will not be possible
  • You will be able to create a new account

Things to Consider

  • Account deletion is irreversible
  • Backup your important data
  • Inform your friends
  • Check your shares on other platforms

  • Right to Complain: Complain to the Data Protection Authority
